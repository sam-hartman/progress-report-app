# Teacher Data Handling Guide

**Quarterly Progress Report Generator**  
**For MCPS Elementary School Teachers**

---

## What This Guide Covers

This guide explains how the Quarterly Progress Report Generator handles your data and your students' data. It is written in plain language to help you understand what information is collected, how it is protected, and what you can do to keep it safe.

---

## What Data Is Collected and Why

The application collects the following information to generate student progress reports:

| What We Collect | Why We Need It |
|---|---|
| **Student first names** | To personalize the progress report narratives |
| **Your name** | To identify you as the report author |
| **School name** | To associate reports with your school |
| **Student work images** | To help the AI understand student progress and generate relevant observations |
| **Your notes and observations** | To provide context for the AI-generated draft |

**What we do NOT collect:**
- Student last names (unless you enter them)
- Social Security numbers
- Home addresses or phone numbers
- Grades or test scores
- Demographic information
- Parent/guardian information

---

## How Your Data Is Protected

### Before AI Processing
When your data is sent to the AI for report generation, **all student names are replaced with anonymous labels** (like "Student A" or "Student B"). The AI never sees your students' real names.

### During AI Processing
The AI provider (Mistral AI) operates under a **Zero Data Retention** agreement. This means:
- Your data is processed and immediately discarded
- Nothing is saved on their servers
- Your data is never used to train AI models
- Processing happens in secure European data centers

### In Your Browser
All data stored in your browser is **encrypted**. Even if someone accessed your browser's storage files directly, they would see encrypted text, not readable student information.

### On the Server
Student work images that you upload are **automatically deleted from the server within 24 hours**. They are only kept long enough to process your request.

---

## How Long Data Is Kept

| Data | How Long | What Happens Then |
|---|---|---|
| Everything in your browser | 90 days from your last use | Automatically deleted |
| Uploaded images on the server | 24 hours | Automatically deleted |
| Your active session | 30 minutes of inactivity | Automatically logged out |
| Data sent to AI | 0 seconds | Never stored by the AI provider |

---

## How to Clear Your Data

You can delete all of your data from the application at any time:

1. Open the application settings menu.
2. Select "Clear All Data" or "Delete My Data."
3. Confirm the deletion.

This immediately removes all student information, generated reports, and your personal data from your browser. This action cannot be undone.

**Note:** You do not need to wait for the automatic 90-day purge. You can clear your data at any time.

---

## What Happens When You Close the Browser

- **Your data stays encrypted in your browser** until you return or until the 90-day automatic purge.
- **Your session expires** after 30 minutes of inactivity. When you return, you will need to log in again.
- **No data is sent anywhere** when you close the browser. Everything stays on your device.

## Best Practices for Data Safety

### Do

- **Use your school-issued device.** School devices have additional security protections managed by MCPS IT.
- **Log out when you are finished.** This ends your session and reduces the risk of unauthorized access.
- **Clear your data** when you have finished a reporting cycle and no longer need the drafts.
- **Review all AI-generated reports carefully** before using them. The AI creates drafts, not final reports. You are responsible for the final content.
- **Keep your browser up to date.** Browser updates include important security fixes.
- **Use the application over a secure network.** Your school's Wi-Fi is appropriate. Avoid public Wi-Fi at coffee shops or airports.

### Do Not

- **Do not use the application on a shared or public computer** (library computers, shared family devices, etc.). If you must, clear all data before leaving.
- **Do not share your login credentials** with anyone.
- **Do not enter more student information than necessary.** First names and relevant observations are sufficient.
- **Do not take screenshots of the application** that show student information and store them in unsecured locations.
- **Do not copy AI-generated text containing student names** into unsecured documents, emails, or messaging apps before finalizing reports.

## Your Responsibilities Under FERPA

As a teacher, you are a "school official" under FERPA (the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act). When using this application:

- You have a **legitimate educational interest** in generating progress reports for your students.
- You are the **data controller** -- you decide what information to enter and you approve all final reports.
- You must handle student data with the **same care** you would apply to any other student record.
- Parents have the **right to inspect** any education records, including progress reports generated with this tool.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the AI remember my students?
No. The AI operates under a Zero Data Retention agreement. It processes your request and immediately discards all data. It does not remember anything from previous sessions or other teachers' sessions.

### Can other teachers see my data?
No. Your data is stored only in your browser on your device. Other teachers cannot access it, even if they use the same application on their own devices.

### What if I accidentally enter sensitive information?
Clear your data immediately using the application settings. The information in your browser will be deleted. Any data that was sent to the AI was anonymized (student names removed) and was not retained by the AI provider.

### Is this application approved by MCPS?
This application has been reviewed against MCPS data handling policies and FERPA requirements. Contact your school administration or the MCPS Office of District Technology for specific approval status.

### Can I use this on my personal phone or tablet?
It is recommended that you use your school-issued device. If you must use a personal device, ensure it is password-protected, uses an up-to-date browser, and that you clear all data when you are finished.

### What if I think there has been a data breach?
Contact the MCPS Office of District Technology immediately. Do not attempt to investigate on your own. Provide details about what you observed and when.

### Can parents request to see the AI-generated drafts?
Under FERPA, parents have the right to inspect education records. If a parent requests to see a progress report, your finalized report is the education record. AI-generated drafts that you did not finalize are working documents, but consult your school administration for guidance on specific requests.

### How is this different from typing a report in a Word document?
The key difference is that this application uses AI processing. To protect student privacy, the application anonymizes student names before sending data to the AI and uses a Zero Data Retention agreement to ensure nothing is stored by the AI provider. Your Word documents stay entirely on your device; this application sends anonymized data to an AI service for processing, but with strong privacy protections in place.
